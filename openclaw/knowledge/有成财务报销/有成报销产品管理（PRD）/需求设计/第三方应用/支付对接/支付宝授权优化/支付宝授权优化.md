1、编辑账户时 ，账号管理页面增加【授权到卡支付】和【授权到户支付】两个操作

![Picture 2](https://alidocs2.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/WgZOZA8Q9Z8o8qLX/img/55cbc8ea-7e03-4fb7-933e-cf5cb74803bf.png?Expires=1775016721&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAI5tKTjg4Kq1HCdBJ8qpSp&Signature=5ezR1sZP4WOcFO9XLdx9bKsoacY%3D "")

![Picture 3](https://alidocs2.oss-cn-zhangjiakou.aliyuncs.com/res/WgZOZA8Q9Z8o8qLX/img/c8a31328-3d82-4e5c-98ec-a4e824ba4770.png?Expires=1775016721&OSSAccessKeyId=LTAI5tKTjg4Kq1HCdBJ8qpSp&Signature=4ciAVTyOmLS225%2FNgsgPFlcx9C4%3D "")
